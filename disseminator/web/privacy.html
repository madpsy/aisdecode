We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

When you send AIS data to us, we collect the following information:

  • AIS Data: Vessel identification, position, course, speed, and other publicly available vessel information.
  • Receiver's Identifier: The identifier of the receiver who sends the AIS data (this could be an IP address or a unique identifier associated with the device).
  • IP Address: Your current IP address, which is used to link the data to the receiver’s location on the map.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected to:

  • Track and display relevant vessel data on a map.
  • Link the AIS data to the receiver’s location for better context and accuracy.
  • Improve the functionality of our services and maintain the quality of the data displayed.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: By sending AIS data and updating your IP address, you are giving us explicit consent to use and display this data.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process the data to improve our services and ensure the accurate presentation of vessel information, based on our legitimate interest in providing these services.

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except for the following:

  • When required by law or a legal process, such as a subpoena or court order.
  • To service providers who help us maintain and improve our platform, with appropriate data protection agreements in place.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. This includes using encryption and secure protocols for data transmission and storage.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: If your data is incorrect, you have the right to have it corrected.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If we process your data based on consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.

7. Retention of Your Data

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. Once it is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize your data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes as necessary.
